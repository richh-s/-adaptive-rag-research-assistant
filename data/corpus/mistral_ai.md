# Mistral AI

Mistral AI is a French AI company founded in 2023 in Paris by Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, and
Timothee Lacroix, three researchers with backgrounds at Google DeepMind and Meta AI. It is one of
the most prominent AI labs based in Europe and has positioned itself as a European alternative to
the primarily US-based frontier labs.

## Flagship product

Mistral's model line includes both open-weight models (such as the Mistral 7B and Mixtral
mixture-of-experts models, released under permissive licenses) and larger proprietary models
(the Mistral Large family) accessed via La Plateforme, Mistral's API service, and Le Chat, its
consumer chat product. Mistral's mixture-of-experts architecture work has been influential across
the broader open-model ecosystem.

## Funding

Mistral AI raised one of the largest seed rounds in European tech history shortly after founding,
and has since raised additional large rounds from investors including Microsoft, reaching a
multi-billion-dollar valuation within roughly a year of founding — an unusually fast trajectory even
by AI industry standards.

## Safety focus

Mistral has generally emphasized open access and European digital sovereignty in its public
messaging, arguing that broad availability of capable open-weight models benefits research and
reduces concentration of AI capability in a small number of US companies, alongside more standard
safety commitments around its proprietary model offerings.
