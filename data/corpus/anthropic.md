# Anthropic

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei, along with
several colleagues who previously worked at OpenAI. The company is headquartered in San Francisco
and was founded with a mission of ensuring that transformative AI is developed safely and benefits
humanity.

## Flagship product

Anthropic's flagship product line is the Claude family of large language models. Claude models are
positioned around a research approach the company calls "Constitutional AI," which trains models to
follow a set of explicit principles rather than relying purely on human feedback for every judgment
call. Claude is available via the Claude API, the Claude.ai consumer chat product, and Claude Code,
a command-line coding agent.

## Funding

Anthropic has raised funding from a mix of venture capital and large strategic partners, including
significant investment from Google and Amazon. The company has consistently emphasized that its
commercial success is intended to fund continued safety research, rather than safety being a
side project of a primarily commercial company.

## Safety focus

Anthropic publishes research on interpretability (understanding what is happening inside neural
networks), alignment, and responsible scaling policies that tie increased model capability to
increased safety commitments. This safety-first framing is the company's primary differentiator
from other frontier AI labs.
